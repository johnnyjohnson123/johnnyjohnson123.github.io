Writing Experience

Hello there and thank you for considering me as a candidate for the position at your company. I have designed this page to serve as a writing portfolio that exhibits three samples of my work. The first piece talks about my introduction to computer science and showcases my deep passion for the discipline, allowing the reader to get a better understanding of what motivates me and why I am a good fit for a software development position.

The next two pieces of work focus on a topic that I am personally very passionate about: increasing the adoption of password managers. While my specialty in computer science does not lie within cybersecurity, I am nonetheless very intrigued about the psychology behind the adoption of technology, and I think it is vital for all software developers to understand that relationship so that they can design more effective applications.

The first of the two pieces focuses on conducting research and exhibiting my understanding of the topic, which reassures the reader that I possess a solid grasp of the topic before proposing any possible solutions or approaches. After consulting with various different studies, I noticed that there were conflicting views on what exactly the best approach was to increasing user adoption of password managers. Having found this, I continued my research and applied my own critical thinking skills to assess what I personally thought to be the most influential strategy possible.

I ultimately concluded that the best approach would be to educate the user about how easy it is to crack passwords and then segue into how password managers can easily address this issue. Thus, my third and final writing sample focuses on a pamphlet geared towards a public audience, where I walk the reader through an effective example and then slowly build towards the use of a password manager.

As you scroll down, you will find small project cards that exhibit three writing samples. I strongly believe that, in addition to my robust behavioral and technical skills, these writing samples will demonstrate my understanding of human psychology and its relevance when designing a digital product.
